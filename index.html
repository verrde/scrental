Time for Sun & Fun! Our very lovely and comfortable villa at the Island Club awaits your arrival! We have a brand new kitchen- it's the best! The beach is one of the finest with wide beaches perfect for walking, biking, shelling, and spotting the dolphins; nature is here. The Island Club is a 30 acre family resort offering 24 hour security, 2 new state of the art pools, with a spa and tanning bed, 9 tennis courts, a playground, BBQ areas, beachfront viranda, fitness center, lagoons for fishing or duck watching, green spaces, and a very relaxed environment. We call it Paradise. We have vacationed here for 40+ years and purchased our condo 21 years ago.

The villa has been totally renovated with wood floors throughout, a soft relaxing color scheme, and some surprises. You will be right at home. Ammenities: 2 bedroom/2 bathroom w/ king bed in master, 2 full beds in the guest bedroom, new air conditioning, 3 cable flat screen TVs, stereo CD player, 2 DVD players, 2 VCRS, wireless highspeed internet access, chairs for the beach, and an umbrella. Full kitchen includes SS microwave, dishwasher, new SS full size refridgerator, SS stove, new high effeciency washer and dryer, fully stocked for dining and cook's tools. The large deck is perfect for morning coffee, viewing the abundant wildlife, and relaxing in the serene environment.
